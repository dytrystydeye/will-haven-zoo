introduction

Step into the wild with our exciting new animal exhibits! Get ready for a roaring adventure as you spot majestic lions lounging like royalty, chuckle at playful monkeys swinging into action, marvel at gentle giants like the awe-inspiring elephants, and gaze at graceful giraffes reaching for the stars. Experience the unexpected as you discover exotic creatures from around the globe, from the vibrant feathers of tropical birds to the slithering charm of mesmerizing snakes. Fun for all ages awaits with interactive displays, engaging animal talks, and hands-on experiences. Capture unforgettable memories at our animal-themed photo booths and share your adventures with friends and family.join us for the opening day festivities featuring face painting, live music, and special giveaways for the first 100 guests!

The Bioluminescent Reef

Prepare to be dazzled by the magic of the deep sea at The Bioluminescent Reef, where nature's most enchanting light show unfolds in a mesmerizing underwater spectacle. Step into an otherworldly sanctuary alive with vibrant colors as bioluminescent sea creatures like glowing jellyfish and flashing deep-sea squid illuminate their surroundings. Discover the science behind these natural light displays through interactive exhibits and simulations that reveal how bioluminescence, produced through chemical reactions, aids in communication, predation, and survival. Immerse yourself in the ethereal beauty of the ocean's most captivating inhabitants and their radiant performances enchant and educate you about the wonders of the deep.


The Arctic Wonderland

"Experience the icy wonder of the Arctic without braving the chill at The Arctic Wonderland, a climate-controlled exhibit that transports you to the frozen realms of the north. Marvel at life-sized replicas of majestic polar bears, playful penguins, and sleek seals in their natural, icy habitats.Engage with interactive displays that vividly illustrate the critical impacts of climate change on these vulnerable regions and explore actionable ways to help preserve their fragile ecosystems. Immerse yourself in the beauty and urgency of Arctic conservation while enjoying a breathtaking journey through one of the planet's most remote and stunning environments."


The Rainforest Canopy

Ascend into the heart of a vibrant rainforest and uncover the incredible biodiversity of this lush ecosystem at The Rainforest Canopy! Walk among life-sized models of exotic wildlife, from vibrant parrots to elusive sloths, and immerse yourself in interactive exhibits that unveil the secrets of this vital habitat. Experience the magic of one of Earth's most diverse and crucial environments as you explore its rich tapestry of life and gain a deeper appreciation for the wonders of the rainforest.
