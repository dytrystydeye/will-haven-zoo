introduction

Welcome to Vancouver Wild Haven Zoo, Vancouver’s newest and most exciting destination for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers! Nestled in the heart of the city,
our zoo offers a unique blend of lush landscapes and diverse animal exhibits. From majestic tigers to playful primates,
Vancouver Wild Haven Zoo is dedicated to conservation, education, and providing an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.
Join us on a journey through the animal kingdom and explore our beautifully designed habitats that celebrate the beauty and diversity of wildlife from around the globe.
Discover, learn, and be inspired at Vancouver Wild Haven Zoo—where adventure meets conservation!

who are we

At Vancouver Wild Haven Zoo, we’re dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences while championing wildlife conservation and education. Here’s what you can expect when you visit:

Explore Diverse Habitats: Wander through our immersive exhibits featuring animals from around the world, from the lush rainforest to the expansive savannah.
Each habitat is designed to mimic the natural environment of the animals, ensuring they live in comfort while offering visitors a glimpse into their world.


Engage in Interactive Experiences: Get up close and personal with our animal ambassadors through our interactive sessions.
From feeding the giraffes to participating in behind-the-scenes tours, these experiences provide unique opportunities to learn about animal behavior and care.


Join Conservation Efforts: Learn about our ongoing conservation projects and how you can help.
We are committed to protecting endangered species and their habitats, and we offer various programs and events that allow visitors to support these crucial efforts.


Enjoy Educational Programs: Our educational workshops and guided tours are designed to inspire and inform visitors of all ages.
